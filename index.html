ACADEMIC EXPERIENCE

Research Scholar | Advisor: Prof. Dr. Fábio Bayer

Statistical Modeling of Digital Images x | September 2022 – August 2023 and September 2024 – August 2025

  • Proposed a new autoregressive model for time series following a reparameterized Maxwell distribution, where the shape parameter is the mean. Parameters were estimated using the conditional maximum likelihood method, with closed-form expressions for the score vector and Fisher information matrix. Model performance was evaluated through Monte Carlo simulations, and practical applicability was demonstrated with real wind speed data from Brasília.

Research Scholar | Advisor: Prof. Dr. Fábio Bayer

Bivariate Dynamic Model for Monitoring Digital Images | September 2023 – August 2024

  • Introduced a new 2D autoregressive moving average model (2D-MxARMA) for modeling the spatial correlation in SAR images. Assumed Maxwell-distributed amplitude values and performed parameter estimation via conditional maximum likelihood. Derived matrix forms for inference and evaluated estimator performance through Monte Carlo simulations.
